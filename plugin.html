ASYG Minecraft Server

Special Commands
/tpa [player] - allows you to request to tp to someone elses location
/ah - allows you to access the auction house where players can bid on items using money
/trade [player] - allows you to trade items and/or money with another player
/sell - allows you to sell your items for money
/bal - see your balance
/kill - instantly kills you when you type it
LWC - Click here for info on how to use the server's chest locking plugin.
/offhand - allows Bedrock players to move items to their offhand
/help - for more info on commands!
